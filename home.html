About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. The game is played on a field with a 22-yard pitch and a wicket at each end (three stumps and two bails).

Key Highlights

  • Objective: Score more runs than the opponent.
  • Teams: Two teams of 11 players (batting & fielding).
  • Pitch: 22-yard strip with a wicket at each end.
  • Batting: Two batters try to score runs.
  • Bowling: Bowlers try to get batters out.
  • Scoring: Runs by running or hitting boundaries (4s and 6s).
